Welcome to Portland's Finest (and Only) Climbing Club!

Come join our enthusiastic climbing community, where our members train hard and enjoy friendly competition. Our main focus is to foster community engagement, to build strong individuals, and to test the limits of our members' climbing abilities.

The Portland Competitive Climbing Club is a free, local climbing cohort that trains members in climbing skills with the intent to join local, and even national, competitions.
